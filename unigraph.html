Unigraphs

Unigraphs is a collection of 555 generative artworks inscribed on palindrome sats on BTC blockchain.
After the initial success of the abstraordinals collection, the goal was to create pieces of generative art that exclusively utilized the unique features of bitcoin and could not be replicated anywhere outside of the bitcoin ecosystem.
On the basis of the uniqueness of sat ID number and text-name, it is possible to create randomized and unique generative art using sat numbers and the bijection function.
This allows the assignment of unique numbers between 0-9 to shapes that always correspond to them and given that each ID number is unique to each sat, each combination of assigned shapes that correspond to them will be unique
Variety on this collection is ensured by having 10 color palettes and 24 shapes sets inspired by de stijl, computer art, folk/tribal art glyphs and patterns, cryptography and abstract modern art.
Cooler sats produce cooler more simpler artworks e.g sat 878678666876878 which is a 3D pali because it has only 3 unique digits.
To optimize file size and loading speed, two techniques (pre-gen and building the art at runtime) were blended.
Each art was randomly assigned shapes for its sat during the pre-gen and inscribed into the browser using recursive inscriptions and JS to build the art piece.
Some artworks have animation traits which were done to use another feature of BTC blockchain-halvings.
